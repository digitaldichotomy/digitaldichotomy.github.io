## OVERVIEW {#overview .text-3xl .font-bold .text-white .mb-6 .text-center}

::: {.grid .grid-cols-1 .md:grid-cols-2 .gap-8}
::: text-gray-300
**THE SILENT HARVEST** documents how corporations have developed
sophisticated, covert systems to extract your physiological data without
consent. This is not privacy violation---it is digital battery.

Through remote photoplethysmography (rPPG), sensor fusion, voice
analysis, and neural inference, companies read your heart rate, brain
state, and emotions through ordinary devices. Your iPhone monitors your
arousal. Your Facebook camera tracks your stress. Your ads target your
depression.

This complete 5-chapter investigation provides the technical evidence,
legal frameworks, and resistance strategies needed to reclaim your
biological sovereignty.
