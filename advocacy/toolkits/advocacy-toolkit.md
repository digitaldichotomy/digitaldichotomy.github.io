## Section 1: Campaign Strategy {#section-1-campaign-strategy .text-2xl .font-bold .dark:text-white .mb-4}

::: space-y-4
::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-2 .border-gray-700}
### Campaign Name & Slogan {#campaign-name-slogan .text-lg .font-bold .text-blue-400 .mb-2}

**Name:** Project Pulse

**Slogans:**

-   \"Your Heartbeat Is Not Their Data\"
-   \"No One Owns Your Nerves\"
-   \"Stop the Harvest. Reclaim Your Body.\"
-   \"Silence Is Sovereignty.\"
:::

::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-2 .border-gray-700}
### Core Message {#core-message .text-lg .font-bold .text-blue-400 .mb-2}

\"Corporations are stealing your biological energy---your heartbeat,
breath, emotions, and brainwaves---without consent. This is not
innovation. This is digital battery farming. And we are ending it.\"
:::

::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-2 .border-gray-700}
### Target Goals {#target-goals .text-lg .font-bold .text-blue-400 .mb-2}

1.  **Immediate:** Ban non-consensual biometric harvesting (rPPG,
    emotion AI, neural inference)
2.  **Medium-Term:** Pass Bio-Physiological Sovereignty Acts in all 50
    U.S. states and major democracies
3.  **Long-Term:** Make bio-obscurity a universal human right under UN
    treaty law
:::

## Section 3: Protest & Civic Action {#section-3-protest-civic-action .text-2xl .font-bold .dark:text-white .mb-4}

::: space-y-6
::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-4 .border-gray-700}
### Protest Actions {#protest-actions .text-xl .font-bold .text-red-400 .mb-2}

::: space-y-4
<div>

#### A. \"Silent Pulse\" Rallies {#a.-silent-pulse-rallies .text-lg .font-bold .text-yellow-400 .mb-2}

**Tactic:** Thousands gather in public squares---no speaking, no
devices.

**Symbol:** Wear red gloves (symbolizing severed sensor lines).

**Chant:**

> \"No eyes on my veins!\
> No AI in my brain!\
> Our bodies are not your grid!\
> We are not your battery!\"

</div>

<div>

#### B. \"Faraday Flash Mob\" {#b.-faraday-flash-mob .text-lg .font-bold .text-yellow-400 .mb-2}

**Action:** In a shopping mall, people simultaneously wrap phones in
Faraday pouches.

**Sign:** \"I am no longer emitting for you.\"

**Goal:** Viral visibility. Media hook: \"The day phones went silent.\"

</div>

<div>

#### C. \"Bio-Denial\" App Boycott {#c.-bio-denial-app-boycott .text-lg .font-bold .text-yellow-400 .mb-2}

**Target:** Apps using rPPG or emotion AI (e.g., Instagram, TikTok,
Alexa).

**Tactic:** Organize mass uninstalls. Track via #UninstallTheHarvest.

**Demand:** \"No bio-harvesting without a real-time indicator.\"

</div>

## Section 5: Defensive Toolkit {#section-5-defensive-toolkit .text-2xl .font-bold .dark:text-white .mb-4}

::: space-y-6
::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-4 .border-gray-700}
### Open-Source Software {#open-source-software .text-xl .font-bold .text-green-400 .mb-2}

-   **/e/OS (e.foundation)** -- Android with granular sensor control
-   **GrapheneOS (grapheneos.org)** -- iPhone-level security, blocks
    covert sensing
-   **Signal** -- Encrypted, no voice analytics
-   **Mullvad Browser** -- Blocks fingerprinting and biometric tracking
:::

::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-4 .border-gray-700}
### Faraday Gear {#faraday-gear .text-xl .font-bold .text-green-400 .mb-2}

-   **Silent Pocket Faraday Pouch** -- Blocks all phone signals
-   **OMode Faraday Hoodie** -- Copper-thread clothing
-   **EMF Shielding Underwear** -- Blocks thermal and bio-emission
    leakage
-   **DIY Option:** Line hats/bags with aluminum foil + conductive
    fabric
:::

::: {.bg-gray-900/50 .p-4 .rounded .border-l-4 .border-gray-700}
### Signal Jamming (Legal in Some Jurisdictions) {#signal-jamming-legal-in-some-jurisdictions .text-xl .font-bold .text-green-400 .mb-2}

-   **LED Face Mask** -- Emits micro-flickers to disrupt rPPG
-   **Voice Modulator Apps** -- Alter pitch/jitter to confuse emotion AI
-   **Touch Dummy Gloves** -- Simulate neutral input during device use
:::
