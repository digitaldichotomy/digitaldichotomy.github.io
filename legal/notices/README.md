# Legal Notices Documentation

## Purpose
This folder contains legal notices and templates for digital battery claims and related advocacy.

## How to Contribute
1. Add new notices as `notice-[description].md`.
2. Ensure compliance with local laws.
3. Use the template:
   ```markdown
   ---
   title: "[Notice Name]"
   ---
   ### Summary
   [Description]
   ```
