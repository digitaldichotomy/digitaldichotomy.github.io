Welcome

This is a space to explore the intricate ways we participate in our own surveillance. In a world of ubiquitous sensors and data collection, the most potent form of monitoring is often the one we consent to, constructing a digital self that is both a mirror and a cage. Here you will find blog posts and publications delving into this modern paradox.
