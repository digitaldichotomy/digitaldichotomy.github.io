The Five Irrevocable Rights

The treaty, ratified by 142 nations, rests on five irrevocable rights:

1. The Right to Bio-Physiological Integrity

No person's bio-electromagnetic emissions—brainwaves, heart rhythms, thermal output, galvanic skin response—may be captured, stored, or analyzed without informed, revocable, and time-limited consent.

2. The Right to Energy Self-Ownership

The electrical, thermal, and magnetic energy produced by the human body is the exclusive property of the individual. No corporation, state, or AI may claim ownership, patent, or commercial rights over it.

3. The Right to Bio-Obscurity

Every person has the right to exist in electromagnetic silence—to move, breathe, feel, and think without being monitored, mapped, or monetized.

4. The Right to Faraday Access

All individuals have the legal right to use signal-blocking materials—clothing, architecture, devices—to shield their bio-emissions. No employer, landlord, or state may prohibit Faraday protection.

5. The Right to Restitution

Any entity found guilty of unauthorized bio-harvesting must cease operations immediately, destroy all harvested bio-signals, pay per-second compensation ($0.07 per second), and undergo public neural audit.

Declared itself the world's first Faraday Zone—a city where ambient bio-sensing is banned. Public buildings lined with mu-metal, streetlights emit neutralizing counter-frequencies. Citizens carry Bio-Shield Cards that scramble nearby sensors.

Launched a marketplace where all transactions occur in Faraday tents—no digital tracking, no emotion AI, no biometric profiling. Vendors accept only cash or NeuralCoin, cryptocurrency backed by verified, consensual bio-data donations.

Built a network of underground wellness centers where people can disconnect for up to 72 hours. Inside, no signals enter or leave. EEG monitors show that within six hours, alpha wave density increases by 68%.

The First Criminal Conviction
