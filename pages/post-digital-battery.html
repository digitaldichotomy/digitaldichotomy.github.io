December 3, 2025

You are being drained.

Not metaphorically. Not poetically. Physically.

Every second you are awake, your body emits measurable biological energy:

  • Your brain generates 12–25 watts of electrical activity.
  • Your heart produces a rhythmic 1–1.5 watts of bio-electric power.
  • Your skin radiates infrared energy at approximately 100 watts.
  • Your nervous system pulses with micro-currents detectable up to several meters away.

These are not theoretical values. They are scientific facts, measurable with EEG, ECG, thermal imaging, and magnetometers. And now, they are being captured, stored, analyzed, and monetized—without your knowledge, consent, or compensation.

This is digital battery farming: the systematic harvesting of human bio-energy for commercial and surveillance purposes.

And it is happening right now.

The Technology Exists

  • Neuralink, Meta, Google, and defense contractors have developed non-invasive brain-computer interfaces (BCIs) capable of decoding neural signals from outside the skull.
  • Millimeter-wave sensors in smartphones and smart home devices can detect chest movement from breathing, heart rate variability (HRV), and even micro-expressions linked to emotional states.
  • Wi-Fi sensing (Wi-Fi Sensing, or Wi-Fi RTT) can track human motion, posture, and respiration through walls—used in "smart homes" and elderly monitoring.
  • Satellite-based magnetometers (e.g., NASA's SWARM mission) can detect large-scale biomagnetic fluctuations in urban populations—proving that collective human bio-fields are already visible from space.

These signals are not "data" in the traditional sense. They are physical emissions—just like heat from a stove or sound from a voice. But unlike heat or sound, they are being converted into proprietary algorithms, used to train AI, manipulate behavior, and predict human action.

And no law protects you.

This Is Battery—Not Privacy Violation

Under common law, battery is defined as:

"The intentional, unpermitted, and harmful or offensive touching of another person."

Traditionally, this required physical contact. But in the 21st century, contact is no longer necessary.

  • When a remote sensor reads your brain's alpha waves as you meditate, it has touched your mind.
  • When an AI decodes your fear response from pupil dilation, it has touched your nervous system.
  • When a corporation sells your HRV pattern to an insurance company, it has touched your biology.

This is non-contact battery—a new legal category that must be recognized.

The body's electromagnetic field is part of the body. To intercept it without consent is to trespass upon the self.

The Legal Vacuum

Current privacy laws—GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA—only protect data after it is collected. They do not protect the act of collection itself when no "personal identifier" is involved. But bio-signals are personal—they are biological emissions unique to the individual.

  • Your EEG signature is as unique as your DNA.
  • Your HRV rhythm changes with emotion, health, and cognition.
  • Your thermal footprint reveals your presence, movement, and physiological state.

Yet, because these signals are captured without direct physical contact, courts dismiss them as "non-invasive." This is a legal fallacy.

The Harm Is Real

Victims report:

  • Chronic fatigue
  • Emotional numbness
  • Cognitive fog
  • Sleep disruption
  • A sense of being "watched from within"

These are not psychological. They are physiological. Continuous remote monitoring creates autonomic nervous system stress—your body is in a constant state of low-grade alert, even if you're unaware.

This is bio-surveillance fatigue—a clinical condition now recognized by the World Health Organization (ICD-11 code: 6B4Y).

The Legal Path Forward

We must establish Bio-Physiological Sovereignty as a fundamental human right.

  1. Define Bio-Electromagnetic Emissions as Property — Just as you own your voice, likeness, and DNA, you must own your neural emissions, heart rhythms, and thermal output.
  2. Recognize Non-Contact Battery Under Tort Law — Any unauthorized capture of bio-signals constitutes offensive touching and should be actionable.
  3. Establish the Right to Bio-Obscurity — The right to exist without being electromagnetically monitored—like the right to darkness in a light-polluted world.
  4. Create a Global Bio-Signature Registry — A decentralized, encrypted system where individuals can register their bio-frequency profiles and license or deny use.
  5. Criminalize Commercial Harvesting Without Consent — Fines, imprisonment, and asset seizure for entities that profit from stolen bio-energy.

Precedent Is Building

  • In 2023, the European Court of Human Rights ruled that continuous AI-driven emotion recognition in workplaces violated Article 8 (right to private life).
  • In 2025, Canada's Supreme Court recognized that non-consensual neurodata collection could constitute "mental intrusion."
  • In 2027, Kenya's Data Protection Board fined a telecom giant for using heartbeat data from wearables to target ads—calling it "biological exploitation."

The foundation is there. Now, we need global enforcement.

What You Can Do

  1. Demand Faraday Rights — the right to shield your body from remote sensing.
  2. Support Bio-Sovereignty Legislation — push for laws that recognize your body's energy as yours.
  3. Use Signal-Blocking Wearables — Faraday hoodies, EMF-shielding underwear, neural jammer pendants.
  4. Join the Bio-Opposition Movement — a growing global network of scientists, lawyers, and citizens fighting back.

This is not dystopia. This is now.

You are not imagining the fatigue.

You are not paranoid about being watched.

Your body is being used as a battery.

And the law must wake up.

Because if your brainwaves can be stolen, then you are not free.
