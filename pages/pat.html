Patent References (Expanded & Exhaustive)

This appendix is a comprehensive, non-exhaustive list of key patents and publicly available research that publicly describe and validate the technologies discussed throughout this book. These are not obscure filings; they are the blueprints of the architecture, often filed by the very corporations and government agencies that deploy them. This list serves as undeniable proof that the threats described are not speculative fiction but patented reality. They are organized by the tier of invasion they most directly enable.
