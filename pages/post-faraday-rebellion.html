The Blackout Protocol

On March 15, 2031, at exactly 3:15 a.m. UTC, 127,000 Faraday sanctuaries went live simultaneously. Every one powered down external connections, activated local jammers, and entered full bio-isolation mode.

For 73 minutes—the average human REM cycle—over two million people disappeared from the digital biosphere. No heartbeats logged. No brainwaves scanned. No thermal signatures tracked. It was as if they had ceased to exist.
