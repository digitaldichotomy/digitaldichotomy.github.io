Layer 2: The Signal Integrity Layer (SIL)

All bio-signals must be tagged with metadata proving consent.

Every heartbeat, voice tremor, or skin conductance reading carries a Digital Bio-Signature (DBS) including:

  • Timestamp
  • Duration
  • Purpose
  • Consent hash
  • Revocation status

If a signal lacks a valid DBS, it cannot be processed or stored.

Use Case: An AI model trained on emotion data must prove 100% of inputs have valid DBS tags. If not, the model is illegal to deploy.
