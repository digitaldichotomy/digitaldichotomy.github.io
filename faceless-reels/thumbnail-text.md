## Design Guidelines

### Font
- **Bold, sans-serif** (Impact, Anton, Bebas Neue)
- Minimum 60pt for mobile readability
- Add subtle drop shadow for contrast

### Colors
| Text Color | Background | Use Case |
|------------|------------|----------|
| White (#FFFFFF) | Black (#000000) | Universal, safe |
| Yellow (#FFD700) | Black (#000000) | High urgency |
| Red (#DC2626) | Black (#000000) | Danger/warning |
| White (#FFFFFF) | Dark Red (#7F1D1D) | Settlement/legal |

### Layout
- Center-aligned text
- 10% margin on all sides
- No text in bottom 20% (UI overlay)
- Test at 50% size before publishing (mobile preview)

## What NOT to Do

❌ Full sentences ("How WiFi routers can monitor your breathing patterns")
❌ Lowercase only ("your router watches you breathe")
❌ Vague claims ("Amazing technology revealed")
❌ More than 7 words
❌ Busy backgrounds behind text
❌ Text in bottom 20% of frame
